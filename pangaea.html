Pangaea

What is Pangaea?

Most people have heard about Pangaea from learning about the Earth's history, learning about basic Geology classes or a general education science class. As of today Pangaea, is an accepted idea but it was not always that way. During the time of this idea, most people couldn't begin to comprehend a supercontinent like Pangaea.

Pangaea was surrounded by a global ocean called Panthalassa and was fully assembled by the early Permian Epoch. This supercontinent began to break apart about 200 million years ago during the Early Jurassic Epoch which eventally led to the current formation of our continents.
